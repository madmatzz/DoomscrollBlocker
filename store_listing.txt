Doomscroll Blocker - Stop Scrolling & Focus

WHAT DOES IT DO?
Doomscroll Blocker is a smart productivity tool and website blocker that physically interrupts you when you have been scrolling for too long. It tracks how far down you scroll on timeline feeds and how many short-form videos you swipe through. When you hit a personal limit, a full-screen block instantly covers your feed. To continue, you must make a conscious choice: either take a mindful pause (Soft Mode) or lock yourself out completely to focus (Hard Mode).

WHY SHOULD YOU INSTALL IT?
If you have ever opened a website just to check one thing, and suddenly realized an hour has passed in a scrolling trance—this extension is for you. 

Modern algorithms are designed to hijack your attention. Doomscroll Blocker gives it back. You should install this to:
- Break Bad Habits: Stop the autopilot cycle of endless scrolling when you are bored.
- Reclaim Your Time: Save hours of lost, unproductive screen time every single week.
- Improve Digital Wellbeing: Limit your daily exposure to endless short-form video feeds.
- Supercharge Productivity: Use the strict "Hard Mode" timer to lock out distracting websites when you desperately need to focus on work or studying.

FEATURES AT A GLANCE
- Smart Scroll Counter: Set a strict scroll limit for timeline-based websites.
- Video Swipe Limiter: Set an exact swipe limit for short-form video platforms.
- Soft Mode (Mindful Friction): Press and hold for 3 seconds to dismiss the interruption.
- Hard Mode (Focus Mode): A strict, timed lock out for when you need deep focus.
- Customizable Timers: Choose between 5, 10, 15, 30, 60 minutes or custom durations.
- Live Stats: Track your scroll distance and video counts right from the extension menu.
- 100% Private: All data stays completely local on your device. No tracking, no external servers.
